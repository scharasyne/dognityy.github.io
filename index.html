    ABOUT US

    Dognity is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to promoting humane treatment for all dogs. This organization is committed to alleviate the stigma against different dog breeds, lessen the number of discriminations against strays, abused, or unadopted dogs.

    Mission

    To protect the welfare of all dogs and promote humane treatment towards them regardless of their breeds.

    Vision

    To alleviate the stigma against different dog breeds, lessen the number of discrimination against strays, abused, or unadopted dogs.

    Our Advocacies

    Social Media

    The advocates will use social media to disseminate and widen the campaign reach where iewers can see updates.

    Posters

    The advocates will utilize posters to catch attention, posted on provincial vets, clinics, along sidewalks, etc.

    Establishments

    Encourage both private and public establishments to protect the welfare of dogs around the area, and avoid hurting them.

    Promote Kapon

    Promote kapon (neutering) to avoid overpopulation, and protect the mama dog's health as well as the puppies' life.
