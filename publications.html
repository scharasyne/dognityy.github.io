    DOGNITY ORGANIZATION BECOMES THE FIRST RECIPIENT OF SARMIENTO NOBLE PEACE PRIZE AWARD

    Dognity is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to promoting humane treatment for all dogs. This organization is committed to alleviate the stigma against different dog breeds, lessen the number of discriminations against strays, abused, or unadopted dogs. As the sole organization in the Philippines to focus on the welfare of dogs, it has become a great achievement for the established management to achieve its first global award, garnering more support from worldwide animal rights advocates, and receiving more help from organizations and people with similar interests, as well as goals in promoting the animal advocacy. Corbie Crizaldo, a youth from England, has expressed her overwhelming devotion for the organization, vowing for wider reach and stronger system in fighting against dog discrimination.

    RC, A DOGNITY RESCUE, HAS FOUND HIS FUREVER HOME

    RC, who has been in the home of Dognity Sanctuary, has found his furever home just recently, February 18, 1999. According to his new owners, they fell in love upon seeing RC, citing their unspoken connection. It can be noted that RC went viral after several dog owners bypassed his presence and was left alone in the adoption convention, leaving the dog sad and lonely.
